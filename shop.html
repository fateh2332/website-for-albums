About the Sound

**Euphoria** is a meticulously crafted R&B album that dives deep into the high-gloss production and complex emotional landscape of modern soul. It explores themes of new love, confident self-discovery, and the intoxicating feeling of pure, unadulterated joy. Every track is a luxurious listening experience, anchored by powerful basslines and sophisticated rhythms.

Steel X (Instrumental)

**Steel X** is a declaration of war on genre boundaries, forging a sound we call "Hardcore Instrumental R&B." This project strips away the conventional vocal narrative to focus entirely on rhythmic and instrumental intensity, featuring distorted 808s and aggressive, unrelenting energy.

Discover **"Midnight Echoes,"** the newest album filled with deep emotion, smooth vocals, and late-night energy. Each track is crafted to take you into a world of atmospheric beats, storytelling, and raw feeling. Whether you're into moody R&B, dreamy synths, or powerful melodies, this album gives you a full experience from start to finish.
